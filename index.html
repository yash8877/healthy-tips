Tips to Stay Healthy & Avoid the Doctor!
🥗 Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
🚶‍♂️ Stay active – aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily.
💧 Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
😴 Get 7-9 hours of sleep each night.
🧘‍♀️ Manage stress through meditation or hobbies.
🦷 Don’t skip your dental hygiene – brush and floss daily.
🩺 Get regular check-ups and screenings.
